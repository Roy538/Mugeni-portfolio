💬 About Lucy.

I'm Lucy Mugeni a passionate, purpose-driven Chemistry and Mathematics teacher at State House Girls High School, where I’ve spent over 5 years inspiring and empowering young minds. My teaching philosophy is rooted in curiosity, clarity, and compassion. I believe every learner has untapped potential waiting to be ignited, and it’s my mission to turn complex concepts into moments of discovery.

One of my proudest achievements is co-authoring the widely celebrated Dymestified Mathematics Revision Book alongside the brilliant Mr. Charles Rang’inya of Sunshine High School. Together, we set out to simplify Mathematics and create a student-friendly resource that bridges confusion and clarity, turning fear into confidence.
